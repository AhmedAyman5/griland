Our Services

Agrochemicals

We offer a wide range of agrochemicals that help protect crops and improve their productivity... Whether you are looking for pesticides, fungicides or weed killers, we have the right options that suit your need.

Advanced Fertilizers

The needs of soil and crops make us offer a range of advanced fertilizers that enhance plant growth and increase productivity. Our products vary between organic and mineral fertilizers for optimal crop nutrition balance.

Seeds

The seeds are imported from the best international suppliers and are distinguished by their ability to adapt to diverse climatic conditions. We provide a variety of vegetable, fruit and grain seeds that ensure high quality crops.

At GRILAND EGYPT, we are committed to provide products that contribute to sustainable success for farmers. We strive to provide the best agricultural solutions through cooperation with major international companies.

Our vision is to support the agricultural sector by providing products that enhance operational effectiveness and efficiency, while considering environmental and social responsibility.
