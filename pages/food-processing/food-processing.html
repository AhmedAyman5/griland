Who We Are

Pure Freshness, Whenever You Need It

Griland Egypt's food processing division is a leading operation in the manufacturing, international trade, export, import, and distribution of premium frozen fruits, vegetables, and food commodities. Our production facilities are located in the heart of Egypt's Nile Delta - one of the most fertile agricultural regions in the world - giving us direct access to the freshest, highest-quality raw materials.

We control the entire production cycle from farm to final product. Every lot is traceable, every process is certified. We use Individual Quick Freeze (IQF) technology to lock in freshness, flavor, and nutritional value - delivering to the EU, Gulf, USA, Russia, and Africa with the same uncompromising standards.

In 2025, our food processing export sales exceeded $10 million USD - reflecting our strong market presence and the trust of global food manufacturers, retailers, and distributors worldwide.

Our Process

From Farm to Frozen in 5 Steps

🌱

STEP 01

Farm Selection & Harvest

We work directly with vetted farmers in the Nile Delta. Crops are harvested at peak ripeness for maximum nutritional value and flavor.

🚛

STEP 02

Field-to-Factory Transport

Cold-chain logistics ensure produce reaches our Kafr El Sheikh facility within hours of harvest, maintaining freshness from field to factory floor.

🧼

STEP 03

Washing & Preparation

Multi-stage washing, peeling, cutting, and blanching. All conducted in our HACCP-certified processing lines with continuous quality monitoring.

❄️

STEP 04

IQF Freezing

Individual Quick Freeze technology locks in freshness, color, and nutrients at -40°C. Each piece freezes separately - no clumping, no quality loss.

📦

STEP 05

Packing & Export

Bulk (1x10 kg), retail (4x2.5 kg / 1 kg / 400 g), and custom private-label packaging. Ready for EU, Gulf, USA, Russia, and Africa.
