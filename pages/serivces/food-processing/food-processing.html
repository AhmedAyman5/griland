About Our Food Processing Division

Together, Let's Give Value to Your Farmland.

Active throughout Egypt since its foundation in 1985, Griland supports farmers and agricultural landowners in the exploitation and development of their land. Our goal is to achieve a triple performance: economic, social and environmental.

GRILAND EGYPT has recently forayed into the business of trading in agricultural commodities and has a select presence across the value chain. We manage all procedures related to export & import processes, logistics, records and certificates as well as the coordination of all service providers involved.

We are growers and exporters of vegetables and fruits varieties including grapes, mangoes, oranges and more. We are leaders in the grapes field especially the Superior and Seedless Flame varieties. We export to the EU and the Gulf area for more than 10 years and hold the Global Gap Certificate.

Our Process

Farm to Frozen — 5 Steps

🌱
STEP 01

Harvest

Crops harvested at peak ripeness directly from our contracted farms in Egypt's Nile Delta region.

🚛
STEP 02

Cold Transport

Cold-chain logistics from field to factory within hours — freshness never compromised at any stage.

🧼
STEP 03

Wash & Prepare

Multi-stage washing, cutting, and blanching in HACCP-certified processing lines.

❄️
STEP 04

IQF Freeze at -40°C

Individual Quick Freeze technology. Each piece freezes separately — no clumping, full nutrients retained.

📦
STEP 05

Pack & Export

Retail, bulk, and private-label formats. Shipped to 20+ countries with full documentation.

International Trade Events

Fruit Logistica — We Are There.

Fruit Logistica is the world's leading trade fair for the fresh fruit and vegetable business, held annually in Berlin, Germany. It is the global meeting point for the entire fresh produce supply chain — from growers and exporters to importers, retailers, and logistics providers.

Griland Egypt participates in Fruit Logistica as part of our commitment to building direct relationships with European importers, distributors, and retail chains. It is where we showcase our fresh Egyptian grapes, mangoes, citrus, and frozen produce range to buyers from over 130 countries.

Being present at Fruit Logistica means our clients get direct access to the global fresh produce market — and global buyers get direct access to Egyptian-grown quality. If you are planning to attend, we would be delighted to meet and discuss your sourcing needs.
