Process

The Production Journey

A seamless, fully traceable pipeline ensuring food safety from the field directly to worldwide retailers.

Harvesting

1. Harvesting

The journey begins in our nutrient-rich soils. Every crop is meticulously hand-picked at peak ripeness to ensure optimal flavor, sugar levels, and maximum freshness.

Sorting & QC

2. Sorting & QC

Harvested produce flows into our facilities where optical grading machines and expert quality-controllers strictly eliminate defects, ensuring only premium sizes proceed.

Cooling/
Processing

3. Cooling/Processing

Within hours of harvest, the produce is hydro-cooled to lock in vitality. Our rapid IQF freezers instantly trap essential nutrients and textures, preserving nature's perfection.

Packaging

4. Packaging

The pristine produce is carefully nestled into hygienic, custom-ventilated cartons and food-grade packaging. This specialized padding prevents transit bruising.

Logistics

5. Logistics

Safely loaded into climate-controlled containers, our fleets dispatch across oceans. Real-time temperature monitoring ensures the harvest reaches optimal destinations worldwide.
